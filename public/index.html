We are a network of groups organizing to provide aid and support to New Yorkers in the midst of the COVID-19 pandemic.

HV Mutual Aid is committed to:

Redirecting people and resources to those at the frontlines who are providing mutual aid, advocacy and services to the hardest-hit communities.

Providing support to local groups leading the mutual aid efforts in their neighborhoods.

Using the HV Mutual Aid network to channel resources and amplifying needs from the ground.

We are everyday Hudson Valleyites working to lift up local organizing, connect people to resources, and build a citywide movement to address this crisis. Our members include parents, elders, immigrants, people with disabilities and chronic illness, caregivers, care workers, organizers, advocates, and anyone else who wants to get involved.
